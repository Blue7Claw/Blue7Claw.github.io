Chocolate Cake

  • Time:1 hour
Ingredients Quantity
White Sugar 2 cups
All-Purpose Flour 1 and 3/4 cups
Unsweetened Cocoa Powder 3/4 cup
Baking Powder 1 and 1/2 teaspoons
Baking Soda 1 and 1/2 teaspoons
Salt 1 teaspoon
Eggs 2
Milk 1 cup
Vegetable Oil 1/2 cup
Vanillia Extract 2 teaspoons
Boiling Water 1 cup

Step 1:Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two nine inch round pans.

Step 2:In a large bowl, stir together the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Add the eggs, milk, oil and vanilla, mix for 2 minutes on medium speed of mixer. Stir in the boiling water last. Batter will be thin. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.

Step 3:Bake 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cake tests done with a toothpick. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.

Tiramisu

  • Time:5 hour
Ingredients Quantity
Egg Yolks 6
White Sugar 3/4 cup
Milk 2/3 cup
Heavy Cream 1 and 1/4 cups
Vanilla Extract 1/2 teaspoon
Mascarpone Cheese 1 pound
Strong Brewed Coffee (room temperature) 1/4 cup
Rum 2 tablespoons
Ladyfinger Cookies 2 (3 ounce) packages
Vanillia Extract 2 teaspoons
Unsweetened Cocoa Powder 1 tablespoon

Step 1:In a medium saucepan, whisk together egg yolks and sugar until well blended. Whisk in milk and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils. Boil gently for 1 minute, remove from heat and allow to cool slightly. Cover tightly and chill in refrigerator 1 hour.

Step 2:In a medium bowl, beat cream with vanilla until stiff peaks form. Whisk mascarpone into yolk mixture until smooth.

Step 3:In a small bowl, combine coffee and rum. Split ladyfingers in half lengthwise and drizzle with coffee mixture.

Step 4:Arrange half of soaked ladyfingers in bottom of a 7x11 inch dish. Spread half of mascarpone mixture over ladyfingers, then half of whipped cream over that. Repeat layers and sprinkle with cocoa. Cover and refrigerate 4 to 6 hours, until set.

Brownies

  • Time:1 hour
Ingredients Quantity
Butter 1/2 cup
White Sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla Extract 1 teaspoon
Unsweetened Cocoa Powder 1/3 cup
All-Purpose Flour 1/2 cup
Salt 1/4 teaspoon
Baking Powder 1/4 teaspoon
For Frosting
Butter (softened) 2 (3 ounce) packages
Unsweetened Cocoa Powder 3 teaspoons
Honey 1 tablespoon
Vanilla Extract 1 tablespoon
Confectioners' Sugar 1 cup

Step 1:Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.

Step 2:In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.

Step 3:Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.

Step 4:To Make Frosting: Combine 3 tablespoons softened butter, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth. Frost brownies while they are still warm.

Enjoy Your Brownies!
